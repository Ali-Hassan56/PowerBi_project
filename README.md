# PowerBi_project
🍕 Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI
An interactive and data-driven Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard developed in Microsoft Power BI. This project provides comprehensive insights into sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, order trends, and product performance throughout the year. The dashboard enables businesses to make informed decisions through dynamic visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs).

📊 Dashboard Overview
The dashboard analyzes pizza sales data and presents meaningful business insights through interactive reports and visual analytics.

🚀 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Metric                   | Value
💰 Total Revenue         | $817.86K
📦 Total Orders          | 21K
🧾 Average Order Value   | $38.31
🍕 Total Pizzas Sold     | 50K

📈 Dashboard Features

🏠 Page 1 — Sales Overview
- KPI Cards displaying Revenue, Orders, Average Order Value, and Pizzas Sold
- Daily Order Trends Analysis (Day-wise Sales Performance)
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis across all 12 months
- Sales Distribution by Pizza Category
- Sales Distribution by Pizza Size
- Total Pizzas Sold by Category
- Interactive Category Slicer for dynamic filtering

🏆 Page 2 — Best & Worst Sellers Analysis
Top Performing Pizzas
- Top 5 Pizzas by Revenue
- Top 5 Pizzas by Quantity Sold
- Top 5 Pizzas by Total Orders

Lowest Performing Pizzas
- Bottom 5 Pizzas by Revenue
- Bottom 5 Pizzas by Quantity Sold
- Bottom 5 Pizzas by Total Orders

💡 Business Insights

🗓️ Customer Ordering Trends
- Sales peak during weekends, especially on Fridays and Saturdays.
- The highest monthly order volumes are recorded in January and July.

📦 Product Performance

- The Classic Pizza category contributes the highest share of total sales and revenue.
- Large-sized pizzas generate the highest sales among all pizza sizes.

🏆 Best Sellers

- Highest Revenue: Thai Chicken Pizza
- Highest Quantity Sold: Classic Deluxe Pizza
- Highest Number of Orders: Classic Deluxe Pizza

❌ Worst Sellers

- Lowest Revenue: Brie Carre Pizza
- Lowest Quantity Sold: Brie Carre Pizza
- Lowest Number of Orders: Brie Carre Pizza
